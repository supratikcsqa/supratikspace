# Creative Pattern Interrupts

## Context
Use this when a strategy feels generic, or when designing ads/content hooks.

## Creative Levers
1. **The Juxtaposition**: Pair two concepts that "shouldn't" go together (e.g., "The Buddhist Stock Market").
2. **Extreme Inversion**: Take the polar opposite of the status quo (e.g., a "Slow Food" restaurant).
3. **The Hidden Truth**: Reveal a dirty secret or a counter-intuitive fact about the industry.

## Cognitive Architecture
- **Tree of Thoughts**: Generate 5 "Safe" ideas → Intentionally discard them → Generate 3 "Dangerous" ideas.
- **Signal Filter**: Remove any cliches found in the top 10 Google search results for the topic.
