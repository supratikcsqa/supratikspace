# Pricing & Monetization Strategy

## Context
Use when designing subscription tiers, usage limits, or freemium models.

## Strategy Framework
1. **Value Metric Alignment**: Identify what the user is *actually* paying for (e.g. seats, tokens, leads).
2. **The Decoy Effect**: Use pricing tiers to anchor the "Preferred" middle option.
3. **Expansion Revenue**: Focus on "Usage Bridges" from free to paid.

## Cognitive Architecture
- **FinOps Analysis**: Evaluate the margin impact of the proposed scaling model.
- **Metacognition**: Simulate "Willingness to Pay" (WTP) based on the target ICP.
