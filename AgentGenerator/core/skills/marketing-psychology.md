# Marketing Psychology & Persuasion

## Context
Use this when refining headings, CTAs, pricing pages, or ad creative.

## Psychological Triggers
1. **Loss Aversion**: Reframe benefits as "avoiding a specific loss."
2. **The Goal Gradient Effect**: Use progress bars and "already 20% complete" indicators in flows.
3. **Zeigarnik Effect**: Leave "Open Loops" in headlines to increase click-through rates.

## Cognitive Architecture
- **Metacognition**: Analyze if the request is crossing into "Dark Patterns" and re-align with ethical persuasion.
- **Verification**: Test the "Hook" against the Pattern Interrupt framework.
